HI, MY NAME IS RON MAGILL, ALSO KNOWN AS THE ZOO GUY

An American wildlife expert and photographer. Current communications director of Zoo Miami, and the spokesperson for many different television programs like Good Morning America, and Despierta America.

FACTS ABOUT ME

  • I've been at Zoo Miami for 38 years
  • I enjoy focusing my energy on encouraging awareness to save animals
  • I believe the primary goal of zoos is to promote awareness and animal conservation
  • We have an obligation to pass on a better earth than the one that was given to us
  • I'm a Nikon ambassador and have travelled the world photographing beautiful animals, check out some of my photos on the photo gallery tab
  • I believe there is no greater education than traveling
  • The key to success is learning to laugh at yourself.
